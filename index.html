GravAdanga

An industrial ecology investment project dedicated to the sustainable recycling and valorization of construction rubble in Senegal.

Project Overview

GravAdanga is an industrial initiative focused on the ecological valorization of construction waste. The project is designed to serve the national market through a structured and efficient processing chain, contributing simultaneously to economic development and environmental protection.

Why Recycle Construction Rubble?

Recycling construction rubble reduces environmental pressure, limits landfill saturation, and transforms waste into valuable resources for sustainable construction.

Strategic Location

The project is strategically located in the Sandiara Special Economic Zone, in the Thiès region, chosen for:

  • Its role as a major logistics hub
  • Proximity to Senegal’s main economic centers
  • Tax and infrastructural incentives offered by the Special Economic Zone

Processing Chain

1. Collection
Recovery of construction and demolition waste
2. Transport
Efficient logistics between collection points and factory
3. Transformation
Industrial processing and material valorization
4. Commercialization
Distribution of recycled construction materials

Positive Impacts

Socio-Economic Impact

Risk Management & Mitigation
